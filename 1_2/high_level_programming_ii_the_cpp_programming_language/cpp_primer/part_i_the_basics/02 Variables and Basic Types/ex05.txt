Determine the type of each of the following literals.
Explain the differences among the literals in each of the four examples:
(a) 'a', L'a', "a", L"a"
char, wchar_t, string, string with wchar_t

char: 1 byte.
wchar_t: 4 bytes.

(b) 10, 10u, 10L, 10uL, 012, 0xC
int, unsigned int, long, unsigned long, octal int, hexadecimal int

int: 4 bytes. signed.
unsigned int: 4 bytes. unsigned.
long: 8 bytes. signed.
unsigned long: 8 bytes. signed.

(c) 3.14, 3.14f, 3.14L
double, float, long double

double: 8 bytes. 10 significant digits. double-precision.
float: 4 bytes. 6 significant digits. single-precision.
long double: 16 bytes. 10 significant digits. extended-precision.

(d) 10, 10u, 10., 10e-2
int, unsigned int, double, double
